Spaghetti

Step one dissolve boullion in boiling water. Cut the spaghetti noodles in half and put them inside the boiling water. Step two heat a can of of corned beef onto a skillet. Add spaghetti sauce onto it and mix it. Once heated the corn beef will break apart and sauce will begin to form. During this add what you want to add more taste. Once noodles are prepared, drain the noodles and add your sauce to it and enjoy!
    8 ounces of spaghetti
    One 12oz of corned beef
