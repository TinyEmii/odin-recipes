Chicken

A delicious grilled chicken recipe for you and your family to enjoy. This dish is easy to make and will certainly be enough to fill your appetite. You could also make a dish on the side if this isn't enough for you. You could also impress your significant other or your date out with your cooking skills by following these steps! Thank you "JCKREP" for sharing this recipe with us :). Without further delay, let's get into the ingredients!

    4 large skinless and boneless chicken breast halves
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    1 teaspoon dried rosemary
    1 teaspoon dried thyme
    1 teaspoon dried oregano
    1 teaspoon chopped garlic
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
    Step 1: Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil grate
    Step 2: Rinse chicken breasts, make sure its dry, and stab it a few times with a fork. After that put chicken into a large resealable plastic bag and put olive oil in it. Shake the bag to coat it with oil, add rosemary, thyme, oregano, garlic, salt, and black pepper. Shake bag to coat the chicken with mixed ingredients.
    Step 3: Grill chicken breasts with preheated grill until juices are clear and read meat thermometer into thickest part of chicken. It needs to be 70 degrees C or 160 F, which is around 10 minutes on both sides.
